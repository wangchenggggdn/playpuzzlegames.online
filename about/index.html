How to Play

Simple Controls

Use your mouse or touch screen to drag and drop puzzle pieces. Intuitive controls make the game accessible for all ages.

Multiple Difficulty Levels

Start with easy puzzles and progress to more challenging ones. Each level is carefully designed to test your skills.

Progress Tracking

Track your completion time and moves. Challenge yourself to improve your performance with each attempt.

Game Modes

Classic Mode

Traditional puzzle-solving experience with various difficulty levels. Perfect for both beginners and experienced players.

  • Multiple piece counts
  • Time tracking
  • Move counter

Challenge Mode

Race against time to complete puzzles. Test your skills under pressure and compete for high scores.

  • Time limits
  • Special achievements
  • Global leaderboard

Pro Tips

Start with the Edges

Begin by completing the border of the puzzle. This provides a framework for the rest of the pieces.

Group Similar Colors

Sort pieces by color or pattern to make it easier to find matching pieces.

Take Breaks

If you're stuck, take a short break. A fresh perspective often helps solve challenging sections.
